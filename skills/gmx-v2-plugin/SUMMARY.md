# gmx-v2-plugin
Trade perpetuals and spot on GMX V2 with leveraged positions, conditional orders, and liquidity pool management on Arbitrum and Avalanche.

## Highlights
- Open and close leveraged positions (long/short) on GMX V2 perpetuals
- Place conditional orders (limit, stop-loss, take-profit) with keeper execution
- Add and remove liquidity from GM pools to earn fees
- Query real-time market data, positions, and pending orders
- Built-in safety checks for collateral minimums and execution fees
- Multi-chain support for Arbitrum and Avalanche networks
- Dry-run capability to preview all operations before execution
- Automatic wallet balance and allowance validation
