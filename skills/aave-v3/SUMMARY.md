# aave-v3
Lend and borrow crypto assets on Aave V3 — the leading decentralized liquidity protocol with over $43B TVL.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to earn interest on Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Base
- Borrow against collateral with variable interest rates
- Monitor health factors to prevent liquidation
- Enable/disable collateral and efficiency mode for optimized borrowing
- Real-time position tracking and reserve rate monitoring
- Automatic token approvals and pool address resolution
- Built-in safety checks with dry-run simulation
- Claim accrued rewards from lending activities
