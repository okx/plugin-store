# aave-v3
Interact with Aave V3, the leading decentralized lending protocol, to supply assets, borrow against collateral, and manage positions across multiple chains.

## Highlights
- Supply crypto assets to earn yield on Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Base
- Borrow against collateral with real-time health factor monitoring
- Automatic liquidation risk warnings when health factor drops below safe levels
- View live interest rates and APYs across all supported markets
- Enable efficiency mode (E-Mode) for higher leverage on correlated assets
- Claim accrued rewards from liquidity mining programs
- Manage collateral settings to optimize borrowing capacity
- Built-in safety checks with dry-run simulation before transactions
