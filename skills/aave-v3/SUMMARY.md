# aave-v3
Lend and borrow crypto assets on Aave V3 — the leading decentralized liquidity protocol with over $43B TVL.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to earn yield across Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Base
- Borrow against collateral with real-time health factor monitoring
- Advanced features like E-Mode for correlated assets and collateral management
- Automatic safety checks to prevent liquidation risk
- Built-in reward claiming and position monitoring
- Seamless integration with onchainos wallet for secure transaction management
- Read-only operations for checking rates and positions without gas costs
- Comprehensive support for all major Aave V3 operations
