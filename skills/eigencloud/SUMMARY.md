# eigencloud
Restake LSTs and ETH on EigenLayer (EigenCloud) on Ethereum mainnet with deposit, delegation, and withdrawal management.

## Highlights
- Deposit LST tokens (stETH, rETH, cbETH, etc.) into EigenLayer strategies for restaking rewards
- Delegate restaked shares to operators running Actively Validated Services (AVS)
- Queue and manage withdrawals with 7-day delay protection
- View comprehensive restaking positions and strategy information
- Support for 11 major LST tokens plus EIGEN token
- Automatic ERC-20 approval management with exact amounts
- Two-step transaction confirmation (preview then broadcast with --confirm)
- Direct integration with onchainos wallet for secure transaction signing
