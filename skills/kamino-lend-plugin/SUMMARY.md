# kamino-lend-plugin
Supply, borrow, and manage positions on Kamino Lend — the leading Solana lending protocol.

## Highlights
- View all Kamino lending markets with current supply/borrow APYs and TVL
- Query your current lending obligations and health factor
- Supply assets to earn yield on Solana's leading lending protocol
- Withdraw supplied assets with dry-run preview capability
- Borrow assets against collateral with dry-run simulation
- Repay outstanding loans with transaction preview
- Supports multiple markets: Main, JLP, and Altcoin markets
- Built-in safety checks with health factor monitoring and liquidation warnings
