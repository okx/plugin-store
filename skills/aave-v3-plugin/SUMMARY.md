# aave-v3-plugin
Lend and borrow crypto assets on Aave V3, the leading decentralized liquidity protocol with over $43B TVL.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to earn interest on Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Base
- Borrow against collateral with variable interest rates
- Monitor health factor to avoid liquidation risk
- Manage collateral settings and E-Mode for optimized borrowing
- View real-time market rates and APYs across all reserves
- Claim rewards from Aave incentive programs
- Automatic token approvals and WETH wrapping when needed
- Built-in safety checks with simulation before execution
