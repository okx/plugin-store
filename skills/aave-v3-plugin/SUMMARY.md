# aave-v3-plugin
Lend and borrow crypto assets on Aave V3 — the leading decentralized liquidity protocol.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to earn yield on deposits
- Borrow against collateral with variable interest rates
- Monitor health factor to prevent liquidation
- View positions and portfolio balances
- Check real-time interest rates and APYs
- Enable/disable collateral and efficiency mode
- Claim accrued rewards
- Support for Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Base networks
