# aura-finance
Deposit Balancer LP tokens (BPT) into Aura Finance for boosted BAL and AURA rewards on Ethereum.

## Highlights
- Deposit Balancer Pool Tokens (BPT) into Aura pools for enhanced yield
- Claim boosted BAL and AURA rewards from staked positions
- Lock AURA tokens as vlAURA for 16-week governance participation
- Withdraw staked BPT from Aura pools when needed
- Check positions and balances across all Aura pools
- Browse available Aura-supported Balancer pools with TVL data
- Process expired vlAURA locks to retrieve AURA tokens
- Two-transaction flows with user confirmation for all write operations
