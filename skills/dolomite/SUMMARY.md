# dolomite
Interact with Dolomite isolated lending markets to supply assets, view positions, and simulate borrowing across Arbitrum, Mantle, and Berachain.

## Highlights
- Supply tokens to earn lending yield on isolated markets
- View current supply and borrow positions across chains
- Simulate borrowing and repayment operations with dry-run mode
- Support for Arbitrum, Mantle, and Berachain networks
- Withdraw supplied tokens with flexible amount options
- Browse all available lending markets with TVL and rates
- Safe transaction preview before confirmation required
- Built-in support for major assets like USDC, WETH, USDT, WBTC
