Supply, borrow and earn yield on Morpho — a permissionless lending protocol with $5B+ TVL.

- Supply assets to MetaMorpho vaults for automated yield earning across curated lending markets
- Borrow assets from Morpho Blue isolated lending markets with competitive rates
- Real-time health factor monitoring to prevent liquidation risk
- Access to $5B+ TVL across Ethereum and Base networks
- Claim Merkl rewards for additional yield on lending positions
- Supply collateral to support borrowing positions with flexible LLTV ratios
- View comprehensive portfolio positions including vault balances and market exposures
- Browse markets and vaults with live APY data for informed decision making
