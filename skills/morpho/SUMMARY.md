# morpho
Supply, borrow and earn yield on Morpho — a permissionless lending protocol with $5B+ TVL supporting both Morpho Blue isolated markets and MetaMorpho vaults.

## Highlights
- **Supply to MetaMorpho vaults** and earn yield with curated risk management
- **Borrow from Morpho Blue** isolated lending markets with flexible collateral
- **Health factor monitoring** to track position safety and liquidation risk
- **Multi-chain support** for Ethereum Mainnet and Base networks
- **Claim Merkl rewards** from liquidity mining programs
- **Browse markets and vaults** with real-time APYs and utilization rates
- **Safe transaction flow** with dry-run previews and user confirmation
- **Collateral management** for supply and withdrawal operations
