# gearbox-v3
Manage leveraged Credit Accounts on Gearbox V3 protocol across Arbitrum and Ethereum networks.

## Highlights
- Open leveraged Credit Accounts with up to 10x leverage on DeFi positions
- Add and withdraw collateral from existing Credit Accounts
- Support for both Arbitrum One and Ethereum Mainnet
- Built-in health factor monitoring to prevent liquidations
- Secure transaction routing through onchainos TEE wallet
- Multiple Credit Manager tiers with flexible debt limits (1K-400K USDC)
- Real-time position tracking and account status monitoring
- Close accounts with automatic debt repayment and collateral withdrawal
