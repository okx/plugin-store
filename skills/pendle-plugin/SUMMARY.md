# pendle-plugin
A comprehensive DeFi plugin for yield tokenization on Pendle Finance, enabling PT/YT trading, liquidity provision, and yield splitting across major EVM chains.

## Highlights
- Trade Principal Tokens (PT) for fixed yield exposure across Ethereum, Arbitrum, BSC, and Base
- Buy/sell Yield Tokens (YT) for leveraged floating yield speculation
- Provide single-token liquidity to Pendle AMM pools and earn trading fees
- Mint PT+YT pairs from underlying assets to tokenize and split yield streams
- Redeem PT+YT combinations back to underlying tokens (especially useful post-expiry)
- Real-time market data including APY rates, liquidity metrics, and trading volumes
- Portfolio tracking with USD-filtered position views and comprehensive asset pricing
- Secure execution with preview-confirm flow and automatic ERC-20 approval handling
