# pendle-plugin
A Pendle Finance yield tokenization plugin for trading PT/YT tokens, managing liquidity, and minting/redeeming PT+YT pairs across Ethereum, Arbitrum, BSC, and Base.

## Highlights
- Buy and sell Principal Tokens (PT) for fixed yield positions
- Trade Yield Tokens (YT) for leveraged yield exposure
- Add and remove liquidity from Pendle AMM pools
- Mint and redeem PT+YT pairs from underlying assets
- Support for Ethereum, Arbitrum, BSC, and Base networks
- Real-time market data and position tracking
- Automated ERC-20 approval handling
- Preview mode with price impact warnings before execution
