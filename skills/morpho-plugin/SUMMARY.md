# morpho-plugin
Supply, borrow and earn yield on Morpho — a permissionless lending protocol with $5B+ TVL.

## Highlights
- **Supply** assets to MetaMorpho vaults and earn yield from curated risk managers
- **Borrow** from Morpho Blue isolated markets with competitive rates
- **Multi-chain support** for Ethereum Mainnet and Base networks
- **Health factor monitoring** to prevent liquidation risk
- **Reward claiming** via Merkl distributor integration
- **Comprehensive position tracking** across Blue markets and MetaMorpho vaults
- **Market browsing** with real-time APYs and utilization rates
- **Safety-first architecture** with dry-run previews and explicit confirmations
