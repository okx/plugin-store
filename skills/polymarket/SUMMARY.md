# polymarket
Trade prediction markets on Polymarket by buying and selling YES/NO outcome tokens on Polygon blockchain.

## Highlights
- Browse and search active prediction markets across crypto, politics, sports, and culture
- Buy and sell YES/NO outcome tokens with USDC.e collateral on Polygon
- Check open positions with real-time P&L tracking and unrealized gains
- Place limit orders or execute market orders with GTC/FOK order types
- Cancel individual orders, market-specific orders, or all open orders
- View detailed market data including order books, trading volume, and liquidity
- Support for both standard and negative risk (multi-outcome) markets
- Automatic USDC.e and CTF token approvals for seamless trading
