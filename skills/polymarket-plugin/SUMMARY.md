# polymarket-plugin
Trade prediction markets on Polymarket by buying and selling YES/NO outcome tokens on Polygon.

## Highlights
- Trade prediction markets with YES/NO and categorical outcome tokens
- Support for both EOA mode (direct trading) and POLY_PROXY mode (gasless trading)
- Real-time market data and order book access without authentication
- Automated EIP-712 signing and credential management via onchainos wallet
- Browse markets by category (sports, elections, crypto) or search by keywords
- Quick access to 5-minute crypto up/down markets for short-term trading
- Position management with profit/loss tracking and winning token redemption
- Deposit and balance management for USDC.e on Polygon network
