# polymarket-plugin
Trade prediction markets on Polymarket — buy and sell YES/NO outcome tokens for real-world events on Polygon.

## Highlights
- Binary and categorical prediction markets with real money outcomes
- Two trading modes: direct EOA trading or gasless proxy wallet trading
- 5-minute crypto up/down markets for short-term price predictions
- Zero-setup API credential derivation from your onchainos wallet
- Order book trading with limit and market orders
- Automatic position tracking and winning token redemption
- Region access verification to ensure compliance with restrictions
- Support for sports, elections, crypto, and breaking news markets
