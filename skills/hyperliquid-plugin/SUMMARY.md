# hyperliquid-plugin

Trade perpetuals on Hyperliquid — check positions, get prices, place market/limit orders with TP/SL brackets, close positions, deposit USDC.

## Highlights
- Trade perpetuals on Hyperliquid DEX with CEX-like speed and on-chain settlement
- Check open positions, unrealized PnL, and margin usage across all markets
- Place market/limit orders with optional stop-loss and take-profit brackets
- One-command position closing and TP/SL management for existing positions
- Real-time price feeds for all Hyperliquid perpetual markets
- Cross-chain USDC deposits from Arbitrum and withdrawals back to Arbitrum
- Spot trading support with perp-to-spot account transfers
- Built-in balance checks and fund management across perp, spot, and Arbitrum accounts
