# moonwell
A Compound V2 fork lending protocol enabling users to supply, borrow, redeem, and claim WELL rewards on Base, Optimism, and Moonbeam chains.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to earn interest and receive mTokens representing deposits plus accrued interest
- Borrow against collateral with overcollateralization requirements
- Claim WELL token rewards from lending and borrowing activities
- Support for major assets: USDC, WETH, cbETH, USDbC, and DAI
- Multi-chain deployment on Base, Optimism, and Moonbeam networks
- Dry-run safety features for borrow and repay operations to prevent liquidation risk
- Real-time market data including supply/borrow APR and exchange rates
- Built-in position tracking to monitor supplied and borrowed balances
