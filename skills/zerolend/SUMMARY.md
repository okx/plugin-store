# zerolend
Supply, borrow, and manage positions on ZeroLend (Aave V3 fork) across zkSync Era, Linea, and Blast networks.

## Highlights
- Supply assets to earn yield on ZeroLend protocol
- Borrow against collateral with health factor monitoring
- Support for zkSync Era, Linea, and Blast networks
- Real-time position and reserve data queries
- Automated ERC-20 approval handling for transactions
- Built-in safety checks and dry-run simulation
- Collateral management and efficiency mode settings
- Reward claiming functionality
