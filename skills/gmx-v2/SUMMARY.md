# gmx-v2
Trade perpetuals and spot on GMX V2 with leverage across Arbitrum and Avalanche networks.

## Highlights
- Open/close leveraged positions up to 100x on perpetual markets
- Place conditional orders: limit, stop-loss, and take-profit
- Add/remove liquidity to GM pools for yield generation
- Support for Arbitrum and Avalanche networks
- Real-time oracle price feeds and market data
- Automated keeper execution system (1-30 second delays)
- Query positions, orders, and funding fees
- Built-in slippage protection and dry-run previews
