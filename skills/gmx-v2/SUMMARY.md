# gmx-v2
Trade perpetuals and spot on GMX V2 with leveraged positions, limit/stop orders, and GM pool liquidity management on Arbitrum and Avalanche.

## Highlights
- Open/close leveraged perpetual positions (long/short) with market orders
- Place conditional orders (limit, stop-loss, take-profit) with trigger prices
- Add/remove liquidity to GM pools and earn fees
- Query real-time oracle prices and market data
- Monitor open positions and pending orders
- Claim accumulated funding fees from positions
- Support for Arbitrum and Avalanche networks
- Comprehensive pre-flight safety checks with balance validation
