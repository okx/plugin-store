# lido
Stake ETH with Lido liquid staking protocol to receive stETH, manage withdrawals, and track staking rewards.

## Highlights
- Stake ETH to receive liquid stETH tokens that accrue rewards daily
- Request withdrawals to convert stETH back to ETH with 1-5 day finalization
- Check real-time stETH APR and balance tracking
- Claim finalized withdrawals to receive ETH back to wallet
- Full withdrawal queue management with status monitoring
- Secure transaction routing through onchainos TEE sandbox
- Support for referral addresses during staking
- Automatic reward compounding with rebasing token mechanics
